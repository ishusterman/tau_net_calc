Car accessibility, Service area maps: To service locations, fixed-time arrival:

The TO service area for the fixed arrival time contains all buildings from which a traveler can reach with a car at least one of the destination buildings, arriving at a certain hour. 

The total duration of a trip cannot be longer than the given maximum travel time. 

If more than one of the destination building can be reached, the route to the building that guarantees the closest to the given arrival time, and the travel time for this route, are stored.
