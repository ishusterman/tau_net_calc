Car accessibility, Service area maps: To service locations, fixed-time arrival

The TO service area for the fixed arrival time contains all buildings from which a traveler can reach with a car at least one of the destination buildings during a certain interval of time. 

The total duration of a trip cannot be longer than the given maximum travel time, and a traveler will choose the fastest route. 

If several destination buildings can be reached, [the fasters route]/[the route with the earliest arrival time] is stored.
