Transit accessibility, Region maps: From service locations, scheduled-based departure

The default FROM region map for the fixed time departure presents the total number of buildings that a traveler can reach by public transport from each of the region’s buildings starting the trip in respect to the transit schedule around the building. 

Additional maps present sum of buildings’ attribute values, for each of the selected attributes, like the total number of accessible jobs. 

The total trip duration cannot be longer than the given maximum travel time.
