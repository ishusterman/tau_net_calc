Car accessibility, Service area maps: From service locations, fixed-time departure:

The FROM service area for the fixed-time departure contains all buildings that a traveler can reach with a car from one or more of the source buildings starting at a given hour. 

The total trip duration cannot be longer than the given maximum travel time. 

If a building can be reached from more than one source building, the route from the building that guarantees the earliest arrival time, and the travel time for this route, are stored.
