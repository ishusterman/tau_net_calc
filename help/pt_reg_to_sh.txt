Transit accessibility, Region maps: To service locations, scheduled-based departure

The default TO region map presents the total number of buildings from which a traveler can reach each of the region’s buildings at a given arrival interval of time by public transport. 

A traveler chooses the fastest trip to get to the destination. Additional maps present sum of buildings’ attribute values, for each of the selected attributes, like the total number of accessible jobs. 

The total duration of a trip cannot be longer than the given maximum travel time.
