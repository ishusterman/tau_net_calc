Transit accessibility, Service area maps: From service locations, scheduled-based departure:

The FROM service area for the scheduled-based departure contains all buildings that a traveler, who knows transit timetable and starts to guarantee the fastest trip, can reach with the public transport from one or more of the source buildings. The traveler starts trip to avoid waiting at the first transit stop.

The trip duration cannot be longer than the given maximum travel time. 

If a building can be reached from more than one source building, the route and the travel time from those that [is the fastest] is chosen.
