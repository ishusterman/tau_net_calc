Transit accessibility, Service area maps, From service locations, fixed-time departure:

The FROM service area for the fixed-time departure contains all buildings that a traveler can reach with public transport from one or more facility buildings starting at a given hour. 

The total duration of a trip cannot be longer than the given maximum travel time. 

If a building can be reached from more than one facility building, the route from the facility that guarantees the earliest arrival time is stored.
