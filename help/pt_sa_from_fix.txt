Transit accessibility, Service area maps: From service locations, fixed-time departure

The FROM service area for the fixed time departure contains all buildings that a traveler can reach with public transport from one or more of the source buildings. 

The total trip duration cannot be longer than the given maximum travel time. 

If a building can be reached from several source buildings, the route with the earliest arrival time is stored.

Two figures can be used: one conceptual and one illustrating that the route with the earliest arrival is stored.
