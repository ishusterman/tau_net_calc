Transit accessibility, Service area maps: To service locations, scheduled-based departure:

The TO service area for the scheduled-based arrival contains all buildings from which a traveler, who knows transit timetable can reach one of the service buildings with the public transport as fast as possible. The traveler start trip to avoid waiting at the first transit stop. 

The trip duration cannot be longer than the given maximum travel time. 

If a building can be reached from several source buildings, the fastest route and the travel are stored.
