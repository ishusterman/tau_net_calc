Transit accessibility, Service area maps: To service locations, scheduled-based departure

The TO service area for the scheduled-based arrival contains all buildings from which a traveler, who knows the timetable of all transit modes, can reach with the public transport from one or more of the source buildings. 

The traveler who knows the timetable plans the trip in a way that the waiting at the first transit stop is zero and does not start the trip earlier than necessary.

The trip duration cannot be longer than the given maximum travel time.

If a building can be reached from several source buildings, [the fasters route]/[the route with the earliest arrival time] is stored.

Two figures can be used: one illustrating the difference between the fixed and schedule-based start time, and one illustrating that the route with the earliest arrival is stored.
