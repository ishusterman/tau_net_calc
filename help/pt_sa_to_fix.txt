Transit accessibility, Service area maps: To service locations, fixed-time arrival

The TO service area for the fixed time arrival contains all buildings from which a traveler, who knows the timetable of all transit modes and adjusts the activities to the transit departures and arrivals, can reach at least one of the destination buildings during a certain interval of time. 

The total duration of a trip cannot be longer than the given maximum travel time, and a traveler will choose the fastest trip. 

If several destination buildings can be reached, [the fasters route]/[the route with the earliest arrival time] is stored.

Two figures can be used: one conceptual and one illustrating that the route with the earliest arrival is stored.
